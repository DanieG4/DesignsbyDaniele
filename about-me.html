Welcome to my story!

I'm currently based in Colorado, though my journey has taken me across various places, often inspired by the beauty of travel and new experiences. From a young age, I found solace in art, using it as a means of expression and coping, a love Ive carried with me ever since. Creative expression has always been a constant in my life—whether through art, music, or the things that stir my soul.

My passion for creativity is fueled by the world around me: hiking in the mountains, the energy of live music, the love and inspiration I find in my relationships, and the endless beauty in everyday moments. I’m constantly inspired by the people I meet, the environments I explore, and the stories that unfold through art.

Professionally, I have studied studio art, further honing my skills through a graphic design program. In my work, I strive to bring fresh perspectives and challenge the boundaries of how we perceive the world. Each piece I create is an opportunity to engage, inspire, and leave a lasting impact.
